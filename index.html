About Us

What is Frontball?

Frontball is a modern racquet sport governed internationally by FIPV, played with high speed and precision against a front wall.

🏛️

Maharashtra Association

Pelota Vasca Sport Association of Maharashtra — promoting Frontball across the state since 2023.

🌍

FIPV Affiliated

Affiliated with the International Federation of Basque Pelota (FIPV), the global governing body for Frontball.

Frontball Categories

Frontball is played in three exciting formats under FIPV international rules.

🏃

Individual

One vs One — the purest test of skill

  • ⚡ Fastest format of the game
  • 🎯 Full court coverage required
  • 🏆 Featured in World Championships
  • 👟 Men's & Women's categories
FIPV Official
👥

Doubles

Two vs Two — teamwork and strategy

  • 🤝 Team coordination essential
  • 🎯 Shared court coverage
  • 🏆 Most popular format
  • 👟 Men's & Women's categories
FIPV Official
🤝

Mixed Doubles

Mixed gender pairs — inclusive sport

  • ♀️♂️ Mixed gender teams
  • 🎯 Strategic play style
  • 🏆 Growing format worldwide
  • 🌟 Inclusive & exciting
FIPV Official
